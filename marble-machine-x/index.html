Marble Machine X

In 2016 bandleader Martin Molin of the band Wintergatan released the "Marble Machine" video. Soon after Martin started working on a new and improved version, called the Marble Machine X. This build is ongoing and is a collaborative effort of about 20 engineers from all around the world, including me. My part of the job consists of converting the digitally composed music into templates to be put on the machine. You can check out weekly video updates here on the Wintergatan YouTube channel.
