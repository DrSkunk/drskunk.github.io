Oreo YouTube counter

Together with comedian Henk Rijckaert I built a giant Oreo for the show called "Standaard Koekhandel" by comedian Lukas Lelie. In that show guests come and review their favourite cookie. Henk and I got together and found it fun to create a giant Oreo one that shows Lukas' YouTube subscribers.
